Services:

All services are customizable - our goal is to take the challenge out of bookkeeping for your business. Below are some of the services we offer:

  • Accounts Receivable - Invoicing/Billing and Payments Received
  • Accounts Payable - Paying & Processing Vendor Bills
  • Financial Account Reconciliation
  • Financial Statement Preparation
  • Payroll
  • Payroll Tax Preparation
  • Sales Tax Preparation
  • End of Year 1099 & W2 Preparation
  • New System Setup Or Existing System Cleanup
  • Quickbooks Desktop (Mac or PC) & Online for remote accessibility

Fees:

All rates are hourly - estimates are available after free consultation. Each situation is different and a free consultation ensures you the most accurate estimate. We are able to take credit cards.
